Click-based mechanics.
Click anywhere for the Character to navigate.
Click on an Enemy for the Character to target and attack.
Click on an NPC for the Character to target and conversate (if they have a Dialogue, the cursor will be indication)
[A][D] keys to rotate the camera.
[Q] to open Quest tab
[I] to open Inventory tab

At the end of the path, outside of the Town, you can transition to the Camp.
